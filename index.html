Sharing and Enjoying the Life and Love of the Lord Jesus Christ with the People of Cork

Cork Baptist Church is an active, Bible-believing church that is committed to sharing the good news of Jesus Christ with the world while serving one another and the wider community of Cork, Ireland.

At CBC, we seek to show that while the Word of God is living and active and exposes our hearts to whom we must give account of our lives (Hebrews 4:12-13), we have a High Priest in Jesus, who sympathizes with our weaknesses, and enables us to receive mercy and grace. All this so that we may hold fast to our confession and confidently draw near to the throne of grace (Hebrews 4:14-16).


As the body of Christ, we are diverse in age, ethnicity, personality, and experience. You are warmly welcome to draw near to the throne with us at all of our gatherings.
