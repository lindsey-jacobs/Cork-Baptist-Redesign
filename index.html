Welcome!

We are an active, Bible believing church, committed to sharing the good news of Jesus Christ.

You will experience a diversity of nationalities, ages and personalities within our body. We seek to live and love as Christ by serving one another and the wider community.

You will be warmly welcomed at any of our services or activities.
